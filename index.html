Your Lord Needs You!

Your Lord has been called to war by the King and hereby summons you to fulfill your duty to him as his subject. You are ordered to fill out this form so that we can organize you into proper ranks and file. Submit the form early and you will get first dibs on looting and pillaging. You would do well to remember that those who do not submit the form at all will be rounded up and placed in the front lines. Also, if you do not know how to write or if you lost your writing arm in the last war you can come down to the castle and we will help you fill out the form. See the front gate for opening hours.
