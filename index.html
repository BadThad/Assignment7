Thy Lord Summons Thee!

Hear ye! Hear ye!

Thy Lord hath been called to war by his majesty The King and hereby summons thee to fulfill thy duty to him as his subject. Thou art hereby ordered to complete this form so that we can forthwith organize thee into proper rank and file. Submit the form early and thou will get first dibs on looting and pillaging. Thou wouldst do well to remember that those who do not submit the form at all will be rounded up and placed in the front lines. Also, if thou dost not possess the knowledge of writing or if thou hath lost thine writing arm in the last war thou art to speedily make thy way to the castle and we will help thee fill out the form. See the front gate for opening hours.
